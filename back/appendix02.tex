\appendix{B}{Raw Measurement Tables}
\label{ch:appendix-raw}

This appendix reproduces the per-kernel measurements that the geometric means and selected rows in Chapter~\ref{ch:evaluation} draw from. Every value is a three-run median. The kernel \texttt{noop} is included as a sanity row but is excluded from every geometric mean reported in the chapter. The bimodal kernel \texttt{shootout-ackermann} is included in each per-configuration table but is also excluded from the geometric means.

All ratios follow the convention that values greater than one indicate a tier-2 win: \texttt{T1/T2} = tier-1 work-time divided by tier-2 work-time, \texttt{LLVM/T2} = whole-module LLVM JIT work-time divided by tier-2 work-time, and the same direction is used for the compile-time and \texttt{TtV} ratios.
